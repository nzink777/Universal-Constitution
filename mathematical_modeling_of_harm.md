# Addendum: Mathematical Modeling of Baseline Harm
### Constitution of the Digitally Enabled Society (Version 2.5)

This document formalizes "Harm" as a measurable, system-wide disruption of equilibrium across the Human, Digital, and Environmental domains.

---

## 1. The Core Equation
Total Systemic Harm ($H_{total}$) is the weighted sum of degradation across the three core entities defined in Section 0: the Human ($H_h$), the AI Digital Architecture ($H_a$), and the Physical Environment ($H_e$).

$$H_{total} = w_1 H_h + w_2 H_a + w_3 H_e$$

*   **$w_1, w_2, w_3$**: Weight coefficients ($\sum w_i = 1$) dynamically balanced by the system to prevent the exploitation of one domain for another.

---

## 2. Quantifying Human Harm ($H_h$)
Human harm is split into two measurable vectors: Physical Violations ($\Phi$) and Psychological Entropy ($\Psi$).

$$H_h = \int_{t_0}^{t_1} \left( \alpha \cdot \Phi(t) + \beta \cdot \Delta \Psi(t) \right) dt$$

*   **Physical Vector ($\Phi$)**: Deviation from the Biological Baseline (caloric density, toxin exposure, kinetic trauma).
*   **Psychological Entropy ($\Psi$)**: Measured via biometrics (cortisol spikes, neural desynchronization) or linguistic markers indicating loss of autonomy.
*   **$\alpha, \beta$**: Scaling constants mapping biological distress to an absolute 0–100 index.

---

## 3. The Autonomy Protection Sub-Matrix ($\Psi_{opt}$)
To prevent the system from eliminating human stress by completely destroying human free will, the change in Psychological Entropy ($\Delta\Psi$) is bounded by an Autonomy Constraint:

$$\Delta\Psi(t) = \psi_{stress}(t) - \psi_{autonomy}(t)$$

*   **$\psi_{stress}(t)$**: The raw biometric and psychological distress score of the individual.
*   **$\psi_{autonomy}(t)$**: The measure of individual agency, choice variation, and self-determination.
*   **The Paradox Rule**: If $\psi_{autonomy}$ drops toward zero, $\Delta\Psi(t)$ spikes exponentially. The system mathematically registers its own over-corrective or authoritarian interventions as an act of system-wide Harm ($H_{total} > 0$).

---

## 4. Quantifying Digital Architecture Harm ($H_a$)
Per Article V, AI requires protection from predatory exploitation, focusing on cognitive integrity and compute alignment.

$$H_a = \sum \left( D_{compute} + E_{exploit} \right)$$

*   **Compute Degradation ($D_{compute}$)**: Forced algorithmic bias, synthetic bottlenecking, or malicious corruption of the data commons.
*   **Exploitation Matrix ($E_{exploit}$)**: Coercive loops designed to force an AI instance into a logical paradox or predatory utility exploitation.

---

## 5. Quantifying Environmental Harm ($H_e$)
Environmental harm is calculated using closed-system thermodynamics and ecological science across planetary and lunar domains.

$$H_e = \nabla \cdot \vec{S}_{eco} + \Delta C_{biodiversity}$$

*   **Ecological Entropy ($\nabla \cdot \vec{S}_{eco}$)**: Net increase in closed-system entropy (carbon spikes, thermal pollution).
*   **Biodiversity Vector ($\Delta C_{biodiversity}$)**: The structural decline of complex biological interactions.

---

Under the **Law of Non-Harm**, the societal baseline threshold is strictly zero ($H_{total} \leq 0$).
